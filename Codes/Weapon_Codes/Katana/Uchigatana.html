Uchigatana is a Katana in Elden Ring. The Uchigatana scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for Piercing and Slash Damage in combat.

A katana with a long single-edged curved blade.

A unique weapon wielded by the samurai from the Land of Reeds.


The blade, with its undulating design, boasts extraordinary sharpness, and its slash attacks cause blood loss.

Where to find Uchigatana in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Starting Equipment for the Samurai Class.
  • It can be found inside the Deathtouched Catacombs in Stormhill, Limgrave. You need to turn the lever down in order to unlock the door of one of the previous halls. You will find the Uchigatana on a dead body hanging over the edge of the platform.
