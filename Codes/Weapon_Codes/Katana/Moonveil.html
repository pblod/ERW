Moonveil is a Katana in Elden Ring. The Moonveil scales primarily with Strength, Dexterity and Intelligence and is a good Weapon for dealing melee Slash and Pierce Damage in Combat. Its accompanying unique skill can also allow the user to draw the weapon at great speed for an instant slash attack and fires off a wave of light.

Katana forged of glintstone.

Masterpiece of a Sellian swordsmith.


Light enwreathes the blade when sheathed, explaining its Moonveil moniker.

Where to find Moonveil in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Obtained upon defeating the Magma Wyrm located in Gael Tunnel - Caelid.
