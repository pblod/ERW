Antspur Rapier is a Thrusting Sword in Elden Ring. The Antspur Rapier scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for inflicting addition scarlet rot buildup in addition to Physical Damage.

Spur of a giant ant which has been fashioned into a rapier. The blade drips with scarlet rot.

Scarlet rot is an old legend, of which Maleigh Marais of the Shaded Castle was a private believer. And indeed, he eventually found his own personal goddess.

Where to find Antspur Rapier in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Dropped by Maleigh Marais, Shaded Castle Castellan, a hostile NPC found in the fields west of The Shaded Castle.
