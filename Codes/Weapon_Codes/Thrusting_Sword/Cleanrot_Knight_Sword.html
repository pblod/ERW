Cleanrot Knight's Sword is the longest Thrusting Sword in Elden Ring despite appearing shorter than the Estoc. Cleanrot Knight's Sword scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity, but in the long run scales higher with Strength rather than Dexterity.

Piercing sword of the Cleanrot Knights who fought alongside Malenia, Blade of Miquella.

Mainly used as a backup weapon, in combination with a long-handled armament.

Where to find Cleanrot Knight Sword in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Drops from most Lesser Cleanrot Knights.
  • The single Cleanrot Knight outside the Prayer Room Site of Grace in Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree is a convenient location for farming.
