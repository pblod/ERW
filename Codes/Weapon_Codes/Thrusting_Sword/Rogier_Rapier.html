Rogier's Rapier is a Thrusting Sword in Elden Ring. The Rogier's Rapier scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a versatile sparring Weapon for landing quick empowered hits.

Piercing sword of superior quality, featuring intricate ornamentation. Signature weapon of the sorcerer Rogier.

High dexterity is required to wield the blade to its full potential, but mastery is a sight to behold, characterized by a flowing style which excels in successive attacks.

Where to find Rogier Rapier in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Given at +8 by Sorcerer Rogier at the balcony in Roundtable Hold after defeating Godrick the Grafted.
