Estoc is a Thrusting Sword in Elden Ring. The Estoc scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity is a good Weapon for dealing melee piercing damage and slashing attacks.

Large piercing sword with a long, hard bladed edge which is also capable of slashing attacks.

For its large size, it can be wielded rather nimbly, leaving little opportunity for the enemy to counter.

Where to find Estoc in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Starting Equipment for the Prisoner Class.
  • Can be purchased from the Nomadic Merchant in Liurnia of the Lakes for 3000 Runes.
  • Can be purchased from Patches at the Scenic Isle Site of Grace for 3000 Runes.
