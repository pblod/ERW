Noble's Estoc is a Thrusting Sword in Elden Ring. The Noble's Estoc scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for easy use or for selling.

Estoc carried by wandering nobles. Made to be easy to wield.

Daubed with gold from tip to tail, it should fetch a good sum.

Where to find Noble Estoc in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Dropped by Wandering Nobles in Limgrave, Raya Lucaria Academy and Altus Plateau, who wear feathered caps and coats.
