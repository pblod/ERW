Executioner's Greataxe is a Greataxe in Elden Ring. The Executioner's Greataxe scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity. Its unique Skill, War Cry, grants the user the ability to temporarily increase attack power.

Bulky cleaver welded to a long haft. Said to have been used to dispatch the remnants of defeated armies, felling them like timber.

Perhaps that is the reason it excels at critical hits.
