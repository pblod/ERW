Crescent Moon Axe is a Greataxe in Elden Ring. The Crescent Moon Axe scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity.

Greataxe with a large, arcing blade and long handle. The "Crescent Moon" of its name is said to have been spurred by nostalgia.

Specializes in sweeping through foes by making use of its long handle.

Where to find Crescent Moon Axe in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Users can get it in Stormveil Castle as drop from axe-wielding Exile Soldiers.
  • There are 2 Exile Soldiers within 30 seconds of Rampart Tower site of grace. Go south to elevator down then turn left twice. In the stonesword key room.
