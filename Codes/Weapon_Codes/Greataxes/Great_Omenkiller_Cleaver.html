Great Omenkiller Cleaver is a Greataxe in Elden Ring. The Great Omenkiller Cleaver scales with Strength and Dexterity, and is a good Weapon for a quality build. It is the only weapon in it's class with inate blood loss buildup, which can further benefit from Affinities that add Arcane scaling.

The blade of this huge, loathsome cleaver comprises a row of amputated Omen horns. Weapon of slaughter wielded by Omen killers.

The hideous horns cause blood loss, adding vibrant colors to the ongoing mayhem.

Where to find Great Omenkiller Cleaver in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Dropped by Omenkiller at Volcano Manor.
  • Dropped by Omenkiller boss at Perfumer's Grotto.
