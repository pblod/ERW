Butchering Knife is a Greataxe in Elden Ring. The Butchering Knife scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for characters with high Dexterity builds. Its skill allow users to increase their attack power and execute strong combo attacks.

Huge carving knife made to cleanly butcher the human body. Signature weapon of the Ogress Anastasia, known to have eaten countless Tarnished while disguised as a Finger Maiden.

Restores a very small amount of HP when it squarely strikes an enemy.

Where to find Butchering Knife in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Dropped by Anastasia, Tarnished-Eater.
