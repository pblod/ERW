Wing of Astel is a Curved Sword in Elden Ring. The Wing of Astel scales primarily with Strength, Dexterity and Intelligence. It is a good Weapon for builds that specialize in Intelligence and Dexterity.

Sword fashioned from a delicate wing, suffused with the magic of the stars.

Strong attack unleashes a wave of enchanted light.


Crafted from a relic of the Naturalborn of the Void who is said to have assailed the Eternal City.

Where to find Wing Of Astel in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Found in a small chest at the top of the ruins in southern Uhl Palace Ruins. Can only be reached if you have access to the northern section of Ainsel River.
