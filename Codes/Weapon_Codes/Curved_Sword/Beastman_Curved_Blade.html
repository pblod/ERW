Beastman's Curved Sword is a Curved Sword in Elden Ring. The Beastman's Curved Sword scales primarily with Strength and with its wild shape and design represents the primal nature of their wielders.

Curved sword forged of dull iron wielded by the beastmen of Farum Azula.


The blade is heavy but well-balanced. Comparatively easy to wield for the damage it delivers.
