Eclipse Shotel is a Curved Sword in Elden Ring. The Eclipse Shotel scales primarily with Faith and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for inflicting the death ailment upon foes followed by a flare if an additional input brings down the armament.

Storied sword and treasure of Castle Sol that depicts an eclipsed sun drained of color. One of the legendary armaments.


In Sol, the sight of an eclipse inspires a dreadful awe, preventing an onlooker from averting his gaze.

Where to find Eclipse Shotel in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Castle Sol: Found at the altar of the church in the southeast section of the castle.
