Bandit's Curved Sword is a Curved Sword in Elden Ring. The Bandit's Curved Sword scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for high dexterity characters who can excel at slashing attacks while spinning.

Curved sword with a light, crescent-shaped blade.

Used by the bandits who ran rampant in the wake of the Shattering.

Most of the blade is now darkened and covered in rust, but with enough dexterity this weapon may still stand up to some use.

Where to find Bandit Curved Sword in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Bandit's Curved Sword can be dropped by the Skeletal Bandits in Limgrave & Altus Plateau
    • One of these Skeletal Bandits can be farmed easily just next to the Church of Pilgrimage Site of Grace.
    • Dropped by a Skeletal Bandit just next to the Hermit Merchant Shack Site of Grace.
  • Get the Sacred Blade Ash of War from the Third Church of Marika, put it on any weapon, and the skill one shots the skeleton at the Church of Pilgrimage. Easy farming even at the lowest levels.
