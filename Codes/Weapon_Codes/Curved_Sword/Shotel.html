Shotel is a Curved Sword in Elden Ring. The Shotel scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for melee attacks and dealing damage with body spins by using the Spinning Slash skill.

Curved sword with a severely hooked blade. Its attacks can slip through an enemy's guard.

Made to hunt down humans, this weapon requires high dexterity to wield efficiently.
