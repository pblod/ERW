Scavenger's Curved Sword is a Curved Sword in Elden Ring. The Scavenger's Curved Sword scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for melee users who want to perform short-reaching heavier hits.

Unique curved sword, notched like shark's teeth.

Weapon carried by corpse pillagers who prowl the sites of old battles.


The blade is tacky with blood and covered in hefty nicks, making it totally uneven. Life can be sinister indeed.

Where to find Scavenger Curved Sword in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Found in Mt. Gelmir, on a corpse near a Grafted Scion across a stone pillar-bridge north west of the First Mt. Gelmmir Campsite site of grace. The corpse closest to the ladder has the blade.
