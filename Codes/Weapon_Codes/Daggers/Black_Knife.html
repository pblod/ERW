Black Knife is a Dagger in Elden Ring. The Black Knife scales primarily with Faith and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for dealing additional Holy Damage and for its Unique Skill, Blade of Death, that allows the user to fire an additional projectile.

Dagger once belonging to one of the assassins who murdered Godwyn the Golden on the Night of the Black Knives.

A ritual performed on the oddly misshapen blade imbued it with the power of the stolen Rune of Death.

Where to find Black Knife in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • A drop from defeating the Black Knife Assassin at the entrance of Sainted Hero's Grave, in Altus Plateau.
