Great Knife is a Dagger in Elden Ring. The Great Knife scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity, and is a good lightweight Weapon.

Large knife with a broad, curved blade.

This weapon is designed to Spinning Slash and rend, inflicting blood loss upon the recipient.

Where to find Great Knife in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Starting equipment for the Bandit class.
  • Rare drop from Demi-Humans wielding this weapon.
    • Craftman's Shack: four Demi-Humans hiding in a flock of sheep north of the Site of Grace.
    • Lakeside Crystal Cave: one Demi-Human hiding in a bush at the start of the dungeon.
