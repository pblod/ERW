Crystal Knife is a Dagger in Elden Ring. The Crystal Knife scales primarily with Intelligence, Strength, and Dexterity, and is a good Weapon for short-range melee combat enhanced with extra Magic Damage.

A knife made by sharpening hard crystal.

Signature weapon of glintstone miners.


Used as a tool to extract glintstone from broken rock, and as such a faint magic enwreathes the blade.

Where to find Crystal Knife in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Found in a chest inside the Raya Lucaria Crystal Tunnel, in a storage area three levels down, guarded by Marionettes.
