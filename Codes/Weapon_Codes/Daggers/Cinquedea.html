Cinquedea is a Dagger in Elden Ring. The Cinquedea scales primarily with Strength and is a good Weapon for short range melee combat. This dagger also has the unique ability to boost the damage of Bestial Incantations when held.

Short sword given to high ranking clergymen of Farum Azula.

Raises potency of bestial incantations.


The design celebrates a beast's five fingers, symbolic of the intelligence once granted upon their kind.

Where to find Cinquedea in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Can be found at the bottom of the exterior structure of the Bestial Sanctum, near a broken rat corpse statue.
