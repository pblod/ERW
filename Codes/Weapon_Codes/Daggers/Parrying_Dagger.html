Parrying Dagger is a Dagger in Elden Ring. The Parrying Dagger scales primarily with Dexterity and Strength and is a good Weapon for parrying enemies as its name states. It is accommodated with the Parry skill and can deflect enemy attacks while potentially providing an opening for your next attack.

A knife with curved handguards. Designed to parry, turning foes' attacks against them.

For master of combat who anticipate every enemy strike and counter accordingly, this weapon is all they need.
