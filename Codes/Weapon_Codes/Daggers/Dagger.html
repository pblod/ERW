Dagger is the basic Dagger in Elden Ring.The Dagger scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for short-ranged melee combat, and for when you need an extra lightweight utility weapon on hand.

A standard dagger with a straight blade.

Though modest in reach and capacity for harm, this weapon is light enough to jab in rapid succession and delivers devastating critical hits.

Where to find Dagger in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Sold by Twin Maiden Husk for: 400 Runes

  • Dropped by Highwaymen and different type of Foot solders
