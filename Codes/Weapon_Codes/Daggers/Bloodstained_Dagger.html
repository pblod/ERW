Bloodstained Dagger is a Dagger in Elden Ring. The Bloodstained Dagger scales primarily with Strength and is a good Weapon for short range melee combat.

Dagger with a bloodstained blade.

Afflicts targets with blood loss.


As blood darkened the dagger through repeated slashing and stabbing, its blade only grew sharper and harder.

Where to find Bloodstained Dagger in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Rare drop from Demi-Human Chiefs
    • There is a Demi-Human Chief sleeping before the wooden bridge leading to the Minor Erdtree in the weeping peninsula.
    • There are 2 of them close to each other in the Hermit Village.
