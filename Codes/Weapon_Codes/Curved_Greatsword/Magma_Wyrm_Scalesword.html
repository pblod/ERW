Magma Wyrm's Scalesword is a Curved Greatsword in Elden Ring. The Magma Wyrm's Scalesword scales primarily with Strength and Faith, with reduced scaling in Dexterity, and is a good Weapon for dealing additional Fire Damage during combat

Curved greatsword wielded by Magma Wyrms. The shape resembles a dragon's jaw and is covered in hard scales.


It's said these land-bound dragons were once humans heroes who partook in dragon communion, a grave transgression for which they were cursed to crawl the earth upon their bellies, shadows of their former selves.

Where to find Magma Wyrm Greatsword in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Drops from Magma Wyrm Makar in Ruin-Strewn Precipice in northern Liurnia of the Lakes.
