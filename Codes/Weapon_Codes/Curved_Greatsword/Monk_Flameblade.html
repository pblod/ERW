Monk's Flameblade is a Curved Greatsword in Elden Ring. The Monk's Flameblade scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for players willing to trade weapon damage for lower weight.

Curved sword with a flickering flame motif.

Wielded by the Fire Monks who came from the Mountaintops of the Giants.


The monks came to the land of Liurnia in pursuit of a fugitive who stole their fire.

Where to find Monk Flameblade in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Dropped by Fire Monks.
