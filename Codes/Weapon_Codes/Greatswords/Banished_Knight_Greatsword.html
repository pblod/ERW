Banished Knight's Greatsword is a Greatsword in Elden Ring. The Banished Knight's Greatsword scales primarily with Dexterity and Strength, and is a good Weapon for most players. But, since it requires no Dexterity investment, it's better as a pure strength weapon.

A finely-crafted greatsword intricately engraved with an ancient motif. Given to knights who, whether by misfortune or misdeed, were forced to abandon their homes. Most of these knights were sent to the fringes,

where they were forced to start anew with only despair for company.
