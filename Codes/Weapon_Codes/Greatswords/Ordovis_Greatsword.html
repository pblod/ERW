Ordovis's Greatsword is a Greatsword in Elden Ring. The Ordovis's Greatsword scales primarily with Strength, Dexterity and Faith. It is a good Weapon for Faith builds.

Greatsword of Ordovis, one of the two honored as foremost among the Crucible Knights.

This sword is imbued with an ancient holy essence. Its red tint exemplifies the nature of primordial gold, said to be close in nature to life itself.

Where to find Ordovis Greatsword in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Dropped by Crucible Knight Ordovis, located at Auriza Hero's Grave.
