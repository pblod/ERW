Blasphemous Blade is a Greatsword in Elden Ring. The Blasphemous Blade scales primarily with Strength, Dexterity and Faith and is a Weapon that deals additional fire damage.

Sacred sword of Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy. Remains of the countless heroes he has devoured writhe upon the surface of this blade.

Now they share the same blood, bound together as family. Some HP is restored upon defeating an enemy.

Where to find Blasphemous Blade in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Trade Remembrance of the Blasphemous with Enia at Roundtable Hold.(Join the Serpent King as Family)
