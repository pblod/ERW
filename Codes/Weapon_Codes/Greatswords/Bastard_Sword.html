Bastard Sword is a Greatsword in Elden Ring. The Bastard Sword scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity is a good Weapon for melee users who want to perform longer-reaching heavier hits.

A heavier sword perfect for those who prefer dodge rather than performing parries. It can be wielded with one hand only, but it's attack will be much slower leaving less room for error.

A large sword with a straight blade.

This heavy sword is normally wielded with two hands, but those with sufficient strength can wield it in one alone.

The hefty blade can be swung into multiple foes at once thanks to the broad, sweeping attack, making fending off crowds all the easier.

Where to find Bastard Sword in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Purchased from the Nomadic Merchant at the Castle Morne Rampart Site of Grace, on the eastern side of Weeping Peninsula, for 3,000 Runes.
