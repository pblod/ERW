Knight's Greatsword is a Greatsword in Elden Ring. The Knight's Greatsword scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for melee combat.

Greatsword bestowed upon skilled swordsmen in the demigods' service, and yet had no rank or title.


Through blackened Featuring a long grip enabling a dual-handed use, this weapon was designed to dispatch larger foes. Being charged with such a duty was undoubtedly a great honor.
