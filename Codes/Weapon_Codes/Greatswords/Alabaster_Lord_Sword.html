Alabaster Lord's Sword is a Greatsword in Elden Ring. The Alabaster Lord's Sword scales primarily with Strength and secondarily with Dexterity and Intelligence.

Greatsword forged from a blue-white meteoric ore.

The blade conceals gravity-manipulating magic.


A weapon unique to the Alabaster Lords, a race of ancients with skin of stone who were said to have risen to life when a meteor struck long ago.

Where to find Alabaster Lord Sword in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Awarded upon killing Alabaster Lord in the Lake of Rot.
  • Awarded upon killing Alabaster Lord in Yelough Anix Tunnel.
