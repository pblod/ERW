Maliketh's Black Blade is a Colossal Sword in Elden Ring. The Maliketh's Black Blade scales primarily with Strength, Dexterity and Faith. A good Weapon for inflicting holy-based damage.

Maliketh's black blade which once harbored the power of the Rune of Death. A sad shadow of its former glory.

After a fragment of Death was stolen on that fateful night, Maliketh bound the blade within his own flesh, such that none might ever rob Death again.

Where to find Maliketh Black Blade in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Trade Remembrance of the Black Blade with Enia at Roundtable Hold.
