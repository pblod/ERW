Watchdog's Greatsword is a Colossal Sword in Elden Ring. The Watchdog's Greatsword scales primarily with Strength and is a good Weapon for cleaving many enemies at once.

Stone greatsword wielded by Erdtree Burial Watchdogs.

Though decorated with the watchman's eye, the pupil was taken by graverobbers and is now hollow, leaving this sword a mere lump of stone.

Where to find Watchdog Greatsword in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Dropped by the sword wielding Watchdogs in the Mountaintops of the Giants.
  • Consecrated Snowfield Catacombs: One sword-wielding Watchdog can be found guarding the Imp Head (Elder), which has a chance to drop the sword.
