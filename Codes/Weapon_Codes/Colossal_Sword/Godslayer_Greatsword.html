Godslayer's Greatsword is a Colossal Sword in Elden Ring. The Godslayer's Greatsword scales primarily with Dexterity and Faith, with mediocre scaling in Strength, and is a good Weapon for inflicting fire-based damage.

Sacred sword of the Gloam-Eyed Queen who controlled the Godskin Apostles before her defeat at the hands of Maliketh.

The black flames wielded by the apostles are channeled from this sword.

Where to find Godslayer Greatsword in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Divine Tower of Caelid: Found in a chest behind the Godskin Apostle's boss arena after defeating him.
