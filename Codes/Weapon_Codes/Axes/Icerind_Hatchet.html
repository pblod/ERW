Icerind Hatchet is an Axe in Elden Ring. The Icerind Hatchet scales primarily with Dexterity, with reduced scaling in Strength, and is a good Weapon for inflicting Frost against enemies both in single target and AoE scenarios.

A hatchet with a frost-coated blade. One of several gifts given by Castle Sol in the distant north.

Known as "freezing fog," the blade is thought to be a dragon's scale. Inflicts a powerful frost effect.

Where to find Icerind Hatchet in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Can be found at Temple Quarter, southeast of the Site of Grace, in a chest located inside the ruined bell tower, adjacent to a ring of flowers.
