Sacrificial Axe is an Axe in Elden Ring. The Sacrificial Axe scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity, and is a good Weapon for for aggressive characters who like to face enemies head on. This weapon allows players to take swing after swing especially with the Skill, Wild Strikes.

Hatchet used in ancient sacrificial rite. A Deathbird is depicted as a malevolent deity.

The power of the rite yet lingers. A small amount of FP is restored upon slaying a foe.

Where to find Sacrificial Axe in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Weeping Peninsula (Nighttime only): Dropped by Deathbird that spawns north from Castle Morne.
