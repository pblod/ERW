Battle Axe is an Axe in Elden Ring. The Battle Axe scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity is a good Weapon for melee users who want to deal considerable damage with each swing.

Usually Slash Damage weapons require Dexterity, but Axes are a great way to perfom great slashing damage using mainly strength.
