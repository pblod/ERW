Iron Cleaver is a Axe in Elden Ring. The Iron Cleaver scales primarily with Dexterity and Strength and is a good Weapon for aggressive fighters who like to face foes head on. This weapon allows players to take swing after swing especially with the Skill, Wild Strikes.

Fairly large iron cleaver, commonly used by the maltreated Misbegotten.

Steeped in resentment, these weapons are swung wildly and relentlessly, often after rushing up to foes.

Where to find Iron Cleaver in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Castle Morne. Dropped by the Misbegotten creatures using this weapon.
  • Leyndell, Royal Capital: Chance to drop from the Misbegotten enemies that can be found at behind the big door.
