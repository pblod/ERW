Warped Axe is an Axe in Elden Ring. The Warped Axe scales primarily with Strength and is a good Weapon for melee users who want to deal considerable damage with each swing.

Oversized double-headed axe with a bizarre, almost melted appearance.

Brute strength is the only thing required to wield this weapon, which appears to have taken quite the beating itself.

Where to find Warped Axe in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Has a chance to drop from Omens wielding this weapon.
  • Can drop from the Omen in the Cliffbottom Catacombs.
