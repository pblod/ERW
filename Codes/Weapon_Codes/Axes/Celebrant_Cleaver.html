Celebrant's Cleaver is a Axe in Elden Ring. The Celebrant's Cleaver scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for those users with high Strength and Dexterity stats.

Cleaver decorated with many-colored fabrics.

Ceremonial tool used by dancers during the festivities of Dominula, Windmill Village.


Crafted from human bone.

Grants trace amounts of runes upon landing attacks.

Where to find Celebrant Cleaver in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Possible drop from celebrating enemies that wield it, in Dominula, Windmill village.
  • Go south from the 'Windmill Heights' site of grace, eventually there are four ladies, two sitting, two dancing, three carry the cleaver.
