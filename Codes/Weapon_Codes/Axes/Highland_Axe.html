Highland Axe is a Axe in Elden Ring. The Highland Axe scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a fine Weapon for combat when an attack boost is needed. The skill that accompanies this weapon can temporarily increase attack power and modify its charging attacks.

Single-sided axe used by the warriors of the highlands.

Brave combatants begin battle by crying out their names. Roars are enhanced by this weapon.

Where to find Highland Axe in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Location: Stormveil Castle.
  • Looted off a corpse inside the fortress of the Grafted Scion. The body can be found resting under the large painting that's at the center of the room.
