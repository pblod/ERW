Jawbone Axe is an Axe in Elden Ring. The Jawbone Axe scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity, and is a good Weapon for for aggressive characters who like to face enemies head on.

Axe made from a herbivore's skull. Weapon of the ancestral followers who disdain metal.

This axe is more of a bludgeon; it forgoes a bladed edge, instead using the beast's molar teeth to buffet foes, dealing strike damage.

Where to find Jawbone Axe in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Can be dropped by Ancestral Follower.
  • There are 8 Ancestral Follower with Jawbone Axe just north then east of the Mausoleum Compound site of grace.
