Club is a Hammer in Elden Ring. The Club scales with Strength and is a good Weapon for melee users who don't invest many points into Strength.

A simple, primitive weapon that requires only brute strength and persistence to hammer your foe into the ground.

A thick, solid lump of wood.

Where to find Club in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Default starting equipment for the Wretch
  • Can be bought from the Nomadic Merchant for 600 Runes. He is located south-east of Coastal Cave.
