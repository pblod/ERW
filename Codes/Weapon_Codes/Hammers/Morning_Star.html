Morning Star is a Hammer in Elden Ring. The Morning Star scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for bludgeoning enemies and dealing high damage and blood loss effects.

Battle hammer comprised of a globe attached to a handle. Though a bludgeon dealing strike damage, the appellative star is covered in spikes which cause blood loss.

Ironic given its graceful name, this weapon often reeks of blood.

Where to find Morning Star in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Weeping Peninsula: Found in a chest inside a broken Carriage just a bit south of the bridge from Limgrave.
