Mace is a Hammer in Elden Ring. The Mace scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for breaking through guards and stances. This weapon is ideal for delivering powerful strikes against enemies with a strong guard.

An iron bludgeon with sharp protrusions.

Striking attacks have few drawbacks, potent as they are at breaking through guards and stances, Most suited to head on slugging matches.
