Godskin Stitcher is a Heavy Thrusting Sword in Elden Ring. The Godskin Stitcher scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a potent Weapon for combat situations where Pierce damage and thrusting moveset will benefit the player.

Elegant piercing sword with a celadon colored blade wielded by Godskin Nobles.


The nobles possess skill with the sword unmatched by any lowborn. Despite its size, successive attacks from this weapon are swifter than the eye can follow.

Where to find Godskin Stitcher in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Dropped by Godskin Noble at Volcano Manor.
