Regalia of Eochaid is a Straight Sword in Elden Ring. The Regalia of Eochaid scales primarily with Dexterity and Arcane and is a good Weapon for Arcane Builds.

Treasured sword of Eochaid, a lesser, long-vanished domain. The copper coloration is not to be confused for rust, but is a conduit for its wielder to move it by their will alone. . Inflicts sleep ailment upon foes.

Swords of Eochaid dance through the skies
