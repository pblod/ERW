Noble's Slender Sword is a Straight Sword in Elden Ring. Noble's Slender Sword scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity, and is a good Weapon for Dexterity builds. It is one of the longest Straight Swords in Elden Ring.

Narrow blade carried by wandering nobles.

Made to be easy to wield.


Daubed with gold from tip to tail, it should fetch a good sum.

Where to find Noble Slender Sword in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Dropped by Wandering Nobles around Limgrave.
  • Dropped by Wandering Nobles in Raya Lucaria Academy.
  • Dropped by Wandering Nobles in Caelid, in the graveyard directly south of the Caelid Highway South Site of Grace.
