Weathered Straight Sword is a Straight Sword in Elden Ring. The Weathered Straight Sword scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good starting Weapon.

Straight sword with a rather short but wide blade.


Simple to wield, it was known as the sword of the people.

Though the Erdtree styling is still visible, time has not been kind

to this sword, now merely a shadow of its former self.
