Warhawk's Talon is a Straight Sword in Elden Ring. The Warhawk's Talon scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for Dexterity builds.

Talon-swords are affixed to the legs of the Stormveil's warhawks, but this one has been repurposed for human use.


The blade is thin and lightweight so as not to obstruct the hawk's mobility.

Where to Warhawk Talon in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Can be found in Stormveil Castle as drop by defeating Warhawks. There are four immediately out the door from the Rampart Tower grace.
