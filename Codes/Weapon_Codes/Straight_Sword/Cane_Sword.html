Cane Sword is a Straight Sword in Elden Ring. The Cane Sword scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity, and is a sword that has an odd design, resembling a cane.

Beloved sword of an aged knight whose last quest was long behind him, repurposed into a walking stick. The tip is rather worn.


Though bent of back and without the brawn required even to raise the sword aloft, still he wished to meet his end with weapon in hand.

Where to find Cane Sword in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Leyndell, Royal Capital, West Capital Ramparts site of grace.
