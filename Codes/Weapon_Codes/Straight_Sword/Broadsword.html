Broadsword is a Straight Sword in Elden Ring. The Broadsword scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for melee combat, and dealing slashing attacks to enemies.

A straight sword with a wide blade, suited to slashing attacks.

Horizontal swings boast wide attack range, making it easier to connect with foes.

Where to find Broadsword in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Starting Equipment for the Confessor Class
  • Can be bought from the Nomadic Merchant for 1800 Runes. He is located south-east of Coastal Cave.
