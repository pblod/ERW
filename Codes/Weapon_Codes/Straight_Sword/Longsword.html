Longsword is a Straight Sword in Elden Ring. The Longsword scales primarily with Strength and is a good Weapon for melee users who want to remain mobile during combat.

A straight sword with a double-edged blade.

In addition to its easily-inflicted standard damage, this well-

balanced weapon can also perform powerful piercing attacks.

Where to find Longsword in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Starting equipment for the Vagabond class.
  • Can be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold for 1,000 Runes.
