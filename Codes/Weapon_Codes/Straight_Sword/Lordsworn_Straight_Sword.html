Lordsworn's Straight Sword is a Straight Sword in Elden Ring. The Lordsworn's Straight Sword scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for strong melee combat.

Well-crafted straight sword with an illustrious design, wielded by regulars of a lord's army.


Though blackened and damaged by years of use, it appears to have otherwise been kept in a servicable

condition, despite the soldiers having long since lost their minds.

Where to find Lordsworn Straight Sword in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Dropped by Godrick Soldier in Gatefront Ruins.
  • Dropped by the headless, undead Soldier at Fourth Church of Marika.
