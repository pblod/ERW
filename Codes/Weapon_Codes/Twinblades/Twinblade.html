Twinblade is the basic Twinblade weapon in Elden Ring. The Twinblade scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity is a good Weapon for melee users who want to perform fast slashes that spin in a larger area of effect.

A sword with blades on both sides of the hilt.

This weapon is difficult to wield, and requires high dexterity.


Can be wielded with a single hand, but its true strength is enabled with dual-handed spinning attacks.

Where to find Twinblade in Elden Ring

Can be found at the following location:

  • Is inside a chest, in the underground cellar, surrounded by four intact walls, just entering Dragon-Burnt Ruins.
